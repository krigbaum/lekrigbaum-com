Books


Don't expect to find many poor reviews on this page. There's no shortage of bad technical books and I don't intend to waste anyone's time just to point out a crappy book. I'm sure exceptions may pop up, but I'd just as soon they don't. Also, don't expect to find many perfect scores. Perhaps I'm overly nit-picky, but no matter how much I like a book, there always seem to be a few flaws to my way of thinking. It could be style, organization, too much detail, not enough detail, blah, blah, blah. Which brings me to the last thing I want to mention. The comments are just my opinion, nothing more. You may love a book I dislike or despise a book that's one of my favorites. I hope what I say here has some value to you, but when it comes to assembling your own library, it's what you think that counts.

Trying to decide on a rating system was very difficult. None of them, including the 10 point system I finally settled on, seemed to be exactly what what I wanted. I decided to use the 10 point system along with the following guidelines to allow for a but more nuance.

Rating Recommendation
9 - 10 Recommended
7 - 8 Useful
5 - 6 Limited Value
< 5 A waste of time
